FAQs

What is the goal of mixed ability dance at Dance Into Space? ▼
Through mixed ability dance we aim to spark conversations that lead to uptake of policies and outcomes that are geared towards optimum inclusive arts advocacy.
What is the mission of Dance Into Space regarding persons with disabilities? ▼
At Dance Into Space we hold a pledge to keep innovating, advocating, and pushing to ensure that persons with disabilities everywhere are fully aware of and able to express themselves, exercise their rights, to access information, education and services; and to live free of violence and cultural discrimination.
Why is disability simulation considered inadequate? ▼
Disability simulation fails to capture the nuance and complexity of living in a disabled body. And it certainly fails to give a deep understanding of systemic discrimination and abuse faced by disabled people.
How does Dance Into Space use dance to promote advocacy and inclusivity? ▼
As Dance into Space, we leverage on this in order to give dance to all, while at the same time, being able to use this very dance to pass advocacy and art-ivism in creativity. Dance has been a creative method of delivering messages pertaining advocacy for a long time now.
What efforts are being made to challenge narrow beauty standards in dance? ▼
The dance industry often places emphasis on physical attributes and aesthetics. However, there are many artists and companies who prioritize creativity, expression, and diversity of movement over physical appearance. There has been a growing movement to challenge narrow definitions of beauty and celebrate a wider range of bodies.
Why is there interest in incorporating an aesthetic of difference? ▼
Recently there has been a growing desire within dance communities to challenge binary thinking by incorporating an aesthetic of difference. The traditional focus of dance on appearance is replaced by a focus on how movement is connected to a sense of self and applied creativity. This notion applies to both able-bodied and disabled dancers.
How can art and dance contribute to healing for individuals with disabilities? ▼
Surrounding people with disabilities with attractive sounds, music, shapes, art, film, dance or a therapeutic space can play a significant part in healing and uplifting them and give them a different perception of the world.
