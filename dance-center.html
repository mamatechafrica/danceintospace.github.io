Siaya Dance Residency Centre

Where Creativity Meets Culture and Nature

About Us

We are a residency center that offers individuals and groups an opportunity to live and work outside their usual environments. Providing time and space for creatives, development workers, and researchers, we help you reflect, research, and produce works of beauty and power.

Guests can explore the rich local culture, immerse themselves in natural locations, and experiment on inspiring sites. Whether you seek a space for creative processes, team building, conferences, or well-being workshops, Siaya Dance Residency Centre is the ideal destination.

Location

Nyang'oma Kogelo, Siaya County. Approximately 45 minutes from Kisumu airport.

Rates

KSH: 3,000/= per person per day (full board).
