About Us

Where Tradition Meets Contemporary Expression

Dance Into Space is a vibrant hub for performing arts, celebrating the beauty of dance and theatre as tools for entertainment, education, and community connection. Rooted in the richness of traditional vocabularies and folk media, we've crafted a distinctive contemporary dance-theatre language. Through this innovative approach, we create performances that inspire, educate, and advocate for meaningful change.

Dance Into Space (DIS) is a premier mixed-able performing arts company, focused on contemporary dance, under the artistic direction of Ondiege Matthew.

Mission

To train artists in dance theatre and produce high-quality works for entertainment, personal development and advocacy.

Vision

To have a society where dance and theatre are embraced and utilized by everyone in all areas of life.

FAQs

How can I join Dance Into Space?

You can reach out to us through our contact form or visit our studio during training hours.

What age groups do you work with?

We work with artists of all ages and abilities. Our programs are inclusive and accessible.

Do I need dance experience?

No prior experience is required. We welcome beginners and professionals alike.
